Rationale

Includes should not have circular references. Circular references between Include files can lead to problems compiling a Program or even the complete inability to do so.

Description

This reports show all the ABAP includes that are part of a circular reference.

Remediation

The best answer to this problem is to reconsider the architecture of the program. Sometimes you can use forward declarations to avoid circular references.
