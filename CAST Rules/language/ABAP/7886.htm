Rationale

Some ABAP statements are provided for internal use only in SAP programs. The SYSTEM-CALL statement interacts with the ABAP kernel. SAP may change these statements at any time without notice, leading to incompatibilities in your program. You should know if and when these statements are being used.

This rule searches for ABAP artifacts containing SYSTEM-CALL statements.
