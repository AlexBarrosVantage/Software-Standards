Description

Avoid Artifacts with High Fan-Out (Fan-Out > 5). The Fan-out of an Artifact is the number of other Artifacts that are referenced in it. In the fan-out of an Artifact, multiple accesses to the same Artifact are counted as one access. PB Artifacts are Methods, Functions, Datawindows, Datastores, Events, ... The threshold level is a parameter that can be changed at will.
