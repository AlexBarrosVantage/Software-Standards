Sections and paragraphs should be located after the first statement calling them

Technology

Cobol

Unified Status

N/A

XXL Status

N/A

Critical Quality Rule

No

Weight in Parent Tech. Criterion

3

Rationale

For maintainability and readability reasons, the source must be correctly structured and the sections and paragraphs should appear after the first GO TO or PERFORM referencing them.

Description

This rule searches for Cobol programs containing sections or paragraphs located before the first statement (GO TO or PERFORM) calling them. There are some exceptions such as DECLARATIVES and INPUT/OUPUT procedures for SORT and MERGE statements. In addition, cycles will generate violations.

Remediation

Review the organization of the code and move the sections and paragraphs after their first call.

Sample
PAR-130-A.
...
PAR-050-A.
    MOVE VAL1 TO RES2.
    ADD 1 TO RES2.
    PERFORM PAR-130-A.
    IF RES2 > MAX-VALUE
        GO TO PAR-050-Z
    END-IF
