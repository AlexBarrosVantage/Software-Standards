Avoid Artifacts with more than 5 parameters (PowerBuilder)

Description

For maintainability and readability reasons, artifacts should not have more than 5 parameters. PB Artifacts are Methods, Functions, Datawindows, Datastores, Events, ... The threshold level is a parameter that can be changed at will.
