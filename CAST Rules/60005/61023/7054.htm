Rationale

Large programs are more difficult to understand, and are a sign of a bad modularity of the code.

Description

ABAP programs should have a reasonable size. This reports shows all the ABAP programs with more than 500 lines of code. 500 being a parameter.

Remediation

Review the design of the program to reduce its size.
